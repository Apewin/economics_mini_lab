Law of Diminishing Marginal Utility

As a consumer consumes more units of a good, the marginal utility derived from each additional unit decreases.

Law of Diminishing Marginal Returns

As more units of a variable input are added to fixed inputs, the marginal product of the variable input eventually decreases.

Law of Increasing Marginal Cost

As output increases, the marginal cost of producing each additional unit eventually increases.

Law of Increasing Opportunity Cost

As more of one good is produced, the opportunity cost of producing each additional unit increases.
